Solar Energy Adoption by Region

The accompanying bar graph provides a visual comparison of solar adoption across different regions, highlighting the percentage increase in solar energy usage. The Y-axis represents adoption percentages, while the X-axis lists regions. The graph illustrates the impact of policy changes and technological innovations on solar energy uptake.

The data represents the annual growth in solar energy capacity (measured in gigawatts, GW) from 2010 to 2023. Starting at 40 GW in 2010, the capacity grew steadily each year, reaching 1400 GW by 2023. The growth highlights a significant increase in global solar adoption, with particularly rapid growth after 2015, indicating stronger investments in renewable energy technologies over the years.
